About Us

Our Mission

At ISL Detection Project, our mission is to develop an innovative and efficient solution that empowers individuals who rely on ISL for communication. By integrating advanced machine learning, computer vision, and speech synthesis technologies, we strive to create a seamless and real-time translation experience that enhances accessibility for everyone.

Our Technology

  • Indian Sign Language Detection: Utilizing state-of-the-art deep learning models to translate gestures into meaningful text.
  • Speech Synthesis: Implementing pyttsx3 for accurate and natural-sounding text-to-speech conversion.
  • Computer Vision: Leveraging OpenCV and MediaPipe for real-time video processing and gesture recognition.
  • User-Friendly Web Interface: Powered by Flask, offering an intuitive and accessible experience for users.
